Hosting


Our hoster collects the following data in so-called log files, which your browser transmits:

IP address, the address of the previously visited website (referer request header), date and time of the request, time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time, content of the request, HTTP status code, amount of data transferred, website from which the request comes and browser and operating system information.

This is necessary to display our website and to ensure stability and security. This corresponds to our legitimate interest within the meaning of Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR.

There is no tracking and we have no direct access to this data, but only receive an anonymized, statistical summary. This includes the address of the previously visited page, the frequency of the pages accessed and the number of unique visitors. We do not combine this data with other data.
